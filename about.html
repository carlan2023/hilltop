ABOUT US

Our Mission

Empowering and transforming the most vulnerable and most unfortunate HIV/AIDS OVCs through the provision of high quality education for better resource exploitation, management and control for improved quality of lives of these OVCs”.

Our Vision

A Community with people living with HIV/AIDS and OVCs who are self-reliant and able to survive without always taking due recourse to charity

We are working within our community to improve their Livelihoods through affordable education

The schools program was adopted a development strategy in order to restore hope and solace in the broken hearts of the HIV/AIDS orphaned and vulnerable children (OVCs) living under difficult circumstances. It is basically intended for (1) - Child survival and (2) - Child development. We serve the most unfortunate and less privileged children in both school sections and they  include among others the HIV/AIDS orphaned and other vulnerable children in the community. We therefore intend to encourage families, communities and external parties to treat all orphans and other vulnerable children with respect insuring that they are not treated as helpless victims but as actors in their own rights. They should be entitled to express their own views, actively participate in decision making process and be actively involved in matters that relate to them. We are inspired at having a community with vulnerable groups of people who are self-reliant, able to survive, sustain themselves, advocate, attain and defend their individual rights without always taking due recourse to charity. We see this as more cohesive arm in the creation of a world with better opportunities, liberties and rights for all. With increased literacy levels among the most unfortunate HIV/AIDS Orphaned and Vulnerable Children (OVCs), better opportunities and liberties are created on to them for enhancement of greater social, economic and political equality and rights and this obviously help move the world towards a more compassionate and socially just world. These children too like other children need love, care, support, food, education and other basic necessities of life. Please, let us work together in partnership and contribute in order to improve the situation under which these children live, study, grow and survive.
