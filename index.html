WHAT WE DO

The Education Centre was established in 1999 as St. Henry's High School Kaboyo, but we have later grown to add a kindergarten and nursey school for elementary education.
St. Henry's High School Kaboyo Education project for the orphaned and vulnerable children.  There are two School sections at the Centre namely; 1- Hilltop Foundation nursery and Primary School Kaboyo for pupils of ages between 2 - 12 years old 2- St. Henry’s high School Kaboyo for Children of ages between 12 - 22 years old                       

Elemenatary School education

That's; Hilltop Foundation nursery and Primary School Kaboyo with children of ages Between 2 - 12 years old and St. Henry's High School Kaboyo with children of ages Between 12 - 18 years old. The program was adopted a development strategy in order to restore hope and solace in the broken hearts of the HIV/AIDS orphaned and vulnerable children (OVCs) living under difficult circumstances. It is basically intended for (1) - Child survival and (2) - Child development. We intend to encourage families, communities and external parties to treat all orphans and other vulnerable children with respect insuring that they are not treated as helpless victims but as actors in their own rights. They should be entitled to express their own views, actively participate in decision making process and be actively involved in matters that relate to them.

Secondary School Education

St. Henry’s High School Kaboyo was started as a development strategy in order to restore hope and solace in the broken hearts of the HIV/AIDS orphaned and vulnerable children (OVCs) living under difficult circumstances. The school has 236 students majority of which are OVCs. Their age range is between 12 – 22 years. The school struggles to reduce illiteracy levels among these HIV/AIDS OVCs. It struggles to provide care, support, canceling and guidance but our services are limited by inadequate infrastructure, facilities, resources and finances. These children like other children need love, guidance, care and support in terms of education, personal scholastic materials, uniform, breakfast, lunch, hygiene education, first aid treatment, medicine, transport and upkeep.

Our Current Child Support Programmes

We have worked with the community to start child sponsorship programmes with in our study curricullum. Below are our current programmes and we want to implememnt more in the future

Sponsor A child

We invite you to join our Sponsor a Child program, a meaningful way to support the education and future of a deserving student. Your generous donation will provide essential financial assistance, covering tuition, school supplies, and other educational needs for children who would otherwise be unable to afford these necessities. By sponsoring a child, you are not only giving them access to quality education but also empowering them to achieve their dreams and break the cycle of poverty. Every contribution, no matter the size, makes a significant impact. Together, we can create a brighter future for these children and their communities. Learn more about how you can get involved and make a lasting difference today.

Community Engagement

We are thrilled to announce the availability of full bursaries for outstanding students at our school. These bursaries are intended to provide complete financial support, covering tuition and other educational expenses, to students who demonstrate exceptional academic achievements, leadership qualities, and a dedication to making a positive impact in their communities. Our goal is to ensure that financial constraints do not hinder talented students from accessing the high-quality education and opportunities our school offers. If you are a driven and passionate learner, we encourage you to apply for this incredible opportunity to join our vibrant and inclusive school community. Apply now and pave the way for a bright and promising future.

Half busaries

We offer half bursaries to students who show outstanding potential and a strong commitment to their education. These bursaries are designed to help ease the financial pressure for families and give more students the chance to benefit from our school's excellent programs. Whether you excel academically, lead in your community, or have a passion for learning, we encourage you to apply. This is a fantastic opportunity to become part of our supportive and dynamic school community. Apply today and take a significant step towards a bright and successful future.
